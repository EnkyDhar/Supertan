Industrial Control

Springer Controls is a Master Distributor of Industrial Control products, located in Yulee, FL. Most items can ship same day!

Effective Temperature Control can extend the life of electrical apparatus, such as transformers, drives, motors, and switchgear. TECSYSTEM is a leader in the industry and supplies temperature controls throughout the world. Controllers with up to 12 channels (inputs) are available. RTD or Thermocouple inputs are available. Very durable innovative electronic designs make the difference between TECSYSTEM and other manufacturers.

  1. Electro-mechanical Contactors and Starters from 6 Amp to 700 Amp with electronic coils as well as wound coils.
  2. Solid-State Contactors, Soft start controls,
  3. Temperature Controls using the latest electronic technology. The electronic temperature controls are designed specifically to improve performance and equipment life. Using RTD, Thermocouple, or IR technology they can be used on Drives, Motors, Generators, and Transformers and exceed the CE requirements of EMI and surge protection.
  4. 22mm and 30mm pilot-devices Complementing the base product lines
  5. DIN rail mounted terminal blocks
  6. Power Disconnect Switches and Cam Switches up to 12 steps, oil immersed switches originally designed by Rowan Controls
  7. Crane & Hoist Controls including Pendant Stations, Rotary Gear Limit Switches and Foot Switches.
